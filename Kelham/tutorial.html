How to use augmented reality

Step 1: Open the homepage, and tap "Go to QR scanner"

Step 2: Point the QR code scanner at a QR code

Step 3:  Press the activate AR button

Step 4: Point your phone camera at the floor. Use your fingers to move/scale the 3D model.
